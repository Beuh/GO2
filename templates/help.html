How do I...

...join a band? Click your name in the upper-right of this window, and select "My Profile." In the "Bands" section, click "Add Band" and select the band of your choice! You'll be a pending member until the administrator for that band confirms your membership. You'll get an email about it.

...change my section? Click your name in the upper-right of this window, and select "My Profile." In the "Bands" section, select your section from the pop-up next to the band. Note that some bands aren't organized by section, so there might not be anything to select. So relax and enjoy the anarchy.

...make a suggestion? I want to hear about it. Also, you can check the issues list in github - what you're wishing for may already be on it.

...contact the administator? Email Here!
