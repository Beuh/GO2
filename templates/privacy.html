{% trans %}Gig-o-Matic Privacy Policy{% endtrans %}

Updated 2017-03-13
Information on the Gig-o-Matic will not be sold or provided to anyone outside of the Gig-o-Matic for any use, except under legal compulsion. Note that other members of your band will see your email address, phone number, and whatever other details you enter into the system. If you set your profile so that information may be shared outside your band, people outside your band may see it.

{% trans %}Gig-o-Matic Security Policy{% endtrans %}

Updated 2017-03-13
The Gig-o-Matic cannot promise super high-security for any of the data. The code is written to follow basic standards, but (as with every other web site) a determined actor will undoubtedly be able to break in and read the content. And because the Gig-o-Matic caters to self-proclaimed "activist" groups, there's a fair chance that somebody, sometime, might want to scrutinize the content. For that reason, without being alarmist, consider this when giving the Gig-o-Matic any information you don't want to see on the front page of the New York Times. Or in a dossier somewhere.
